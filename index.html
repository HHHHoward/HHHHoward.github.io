📖 How to Play
  • 🧠Answer the Quiz at the bottom to unlock your first plant!
  • 🌱Once unlocked, pick that plant from the sidebar and tap an empty plot to plant it.
  • 💧Tap "Water All" to help your plants grow through their stages.
  • 🌾When a plant is fully grown, its plot will glow — tap "Harvest" to collect it!
  • 🪙Harvested crops earn you coins — use them to unlock the next quiz and plant!
  • 🗑Tap a planted plot then "Remove" to clear it if you change your mind.
